Challenge Details

Web Dev Challenge Hackathon 5: The Local Food Scene

Build an app to help out your local food scene in any way you can. Apps due Sep 30.

Date Created: 2024-09-26

Difficulty: Medium

Prompt

Make something that gets people excited about their local food scene.

The way restaurants run changed in a big way during the pandemic. The big food delivery apps control which restaurants get seen, and social media algorithm changes make it harder than ever for these businesses to get noticed. Your challenge is to come up with some way of getting more people to notice — and get involved in — their local food scene. This can be anything web-based, whether you want to create some way for people to discover new restaurants and chefs, create an event or a social game, or even tackle the notoriously awful restaurant website design aesthetic.

The rules and how to Submit

Steps to follow:

  • Build a web app that meets the challenge
  • Spend 30 minutes planning your app
  • Spend 4 hours building your app
  • Use Convex, Clerk, and/or Raycast to build your web app
  • Publish the source code as a public GitHub repo
  • Publish the web app to a public URL
  • Submit your web app by 11:59 pm Pacific on Monday, September 30, 2024

Rules

Apps must use Convex, Clerk, and/or Raycast as part of the build.
